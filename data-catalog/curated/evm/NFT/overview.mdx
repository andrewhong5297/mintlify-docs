Non-fungible tokens (NFTs) have emerged as a major force in the crypto ecosystem, enabling unique digital assets and collectibles. Dune provides comprehensive coverage of NFT market activity through curated tables that capture trades, mints, and wash trading detection. These datasets enable analysis of sales volumes, collection trends, minting patterns, and market manipulation across major NFT marketplaces and networks.
