We recognize that getting started with Dune Datashare can be daunting. With our extensive data catalog, identifying if Datashare is for you can be feel next to impossible. With this in mind, Dune has developed trial data tables on Snowflake that is available to any Dune user. Follow the guide below to setup and take part in the trial!

## Accessing the Trial Data

<Warning>
  To access the trial data, you will need to have an account on Snowflake. Please create an account on Snowflake before following this guide.
</Warning>
